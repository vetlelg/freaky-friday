Chicken Stir Fry with Hoisin sauce

Ingredients

Portions: 2

Price: About 120kr

  • 300g chicken fillet
  • 0,7 dl Hoi Sin sauce
  • 300 g classic wok vegetables
  • 250 g egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon rapeseed oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

Time: Less than 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

  1. Cut the chicken fillets into strips and fry in oil until golden all around. Take the chicken out, put in the vegetables and stir fry them while stirring for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Boil a saucepan of lightly salted water and cook the noodles according to the instructions on the package. Pour off the water and steam the noodles dry.
  3. Put the noodles in the wok together with the chicken, the vegetables and finally the wok sauce. Cook until the chicken is just cooked through and the vegetables are just tender. This takes 3-5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper if necessary.
