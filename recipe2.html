Fried Cod with garlic butter and sweet potato

Ingredients

Portions: 2

Price: About 50kr

Time: Less than 20 minutes

  • 1 sweet potate
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 400g cod fillet
  • 1 package of pea puree
  • 65g garlic butter

Directions

  1. Begin slicing the sweet potato in pieces of about 0,5 cm. Add olive oil, salt and pepper to the potato slices. Add the slices in a ovenproof dish and fry in the middle of the oven on 200 degrees for about 15 minutes.
  2. White the potatoes fry in the oven, fry the cod in garlic butter in a pan for about 10 minutes until it's well done.
  3. Cook the pea puree in boiling water for 10 minutes
