Carrot Soup with chili, orange and ginger

Ingredients

Portions: 2

Price: About 40kr

  • 175g carrots
  • 0,5 small onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 0,5 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 0,5 red chili
  • 1,5 dl pressed orange juice
  • 2,5 dl vegetable juice
  • 1,5 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

Time: 20 - 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

  1. Chop onion and garlic. Divide the chili lengthwise and scrape out the seeds. Cut the chili into thin strips. Wash the carrots well and cut them into pieces.
  2. Heat up the oil in a saucepan and fry the onion, garlic, chili, carrot and ginger for a few minutes while stirring. The onion should be shiny without getting any color.
  3. Add broth and orange juice. Put the lid on and let the soup simmer for approx. 20 minutes.
  4. Run the soup smooth and even in a fast mixer and season with salt and pepper. The soup can be served hot or cold.
