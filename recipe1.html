Vegetarian Stir Fry with broccoli, beans and grilled peppers

Ingredients

Portions: 2

Price: About 60kr

Time: Less than 20 minutes

  • 1 broccoli
  • 1 canned beans
  • Half a glass of grilled peppers
  • Half chili
  • Half red onion
  • 1 tablespoon rapeseed oil
  • 1 packet of noodles
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Fry broccoli, beans, peppers, chili and onion in oil until the broccoli is tender, but still has a solid core.
  2. Boil noodles as instructed on the packaging.
  3. Taste the dish and add salt and pepper. You can add a sauce as well if you would like some extra taste. For example Hoisin or Szechuan sauce.
