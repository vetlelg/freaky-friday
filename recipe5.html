Fried salmon with sweet potato puree and broccoli salad

Ingredients

Portions: 2

Price: About 100kr

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 30g herb butter with spring onions
  • 300g broccoli salad
  • 1 package of mashed sweet potato
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

Time: Less than 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

  1. Put the bag of sweet potato puree in lightly boiling water for 10 minutes until it is thoroughly warm.
  2. Season the fish with salt and pepper. Fry the fillets in the ramson butter in a frying pan or on the grill, until the fish is golden on both sides and well cooked. This takes about 4 minutes on each side.
  3. Serve the fried salmon with sweet potato puree, broccoli salad and the herb butter you fried the fish in.
